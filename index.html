‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find coffee shops near me?

Open the app and allow location access. The app will automatically show nearby coffee shops on the map and in the list view.

How do I save a coffee shop as a favorite?

Tap the heart icon on any coffee shop card to add it to your favorites. You can view all your favorites in the Favorites tab.

Why isn't the app showing my location?

Make sure you've granted location permissions to the app in your device settings. You can also try restarting the app or checking your internet connection.

How do I get directions to a coffee shop?

Tap on any coffee shop card and then tap the "Directions" button. This will open your default maps app with turn-by-turn directions.

Can I suggest a new coffee shop to be added?

Yes! Please email us with the coffee shop details including name, address, and any other relevant information.
