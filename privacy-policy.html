Coffee On The Go

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Last Updated: January 2025

Ali Mehran ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Coffee On The Go mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

Location Information

We collect location data to provide you with nearby coffee shop recommendations. This includes:

  • Your current GPS coordinates when you use the app
  • Location data to calculate distances to coffee shops
  • Location data to provide directions and navigation

Usage Information

We may collect information about how you use our app, including:

  • Coffee shops you view or favorite
  • Search queries and preferences
  • App usage patterns and features accessed

Device Information

We may collect device-specific information such as:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Mobile network information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Location Services: To find and display nearby coffee shops
  • Navigation: To provide directions to coffee shops
  • Personalization: To remember your favorite coffee shops
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our service
  • Support: To provide customer support and respond to inquiries

Data Storage and Security

Your privacy and data security are important to us:

  • Local Storage: Most of your data (favorites, preferences) is stored locally on your device
  • No Personal Accounts: We don't require you to create accounts or provide personal information
  • Location Privacy: Location data is used only for finding nearby coffee shops and is not stored permanently
  • Security Measures: We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Maps Services: For displaying maps and providing directions
  • Analytics: To understand app usage and improve performance
  • Advertising: We may display ads through third-party advertising networks

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Location Access: You can disable location access in your device settings
  • Data Deletion: You can delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics and advertising in your device settings
  • Contact Us: You can contact us to request information about your data

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
